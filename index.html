Experience

My Experiences

Internship at Gradient.io Summer of 2020

I had the incredible privilege of working with the Gradient.io team. Gradient.io is a tech startup that is revolutionizing the way Amazon sellers position their products. My project was to Design and Build a browser extension that assists these sellers as they navigate and conduct research on the Amazon website.

Internship at Cocoa Press January of 2020

I worked closely with Ellie Weinstein, founder and CEO of Cocoa Press, on her chocolate 3D printer start-up. I helped her complete a valentine's day event by discussing marketing strategies, 3D modeling all orders, designing packaging, and printing out the majority of chocolates.

Counselor at GFS computer summer camp 2018-NOW

I work with 3rd and 7th graders in hopes of inspiring them to love and understand coding. Computers are such an integral part of our lives; it would be a shame if the leaders of tomorrow were oblivious as to how they work.

Assistant teacher at GFS robotics club 2017-NOW

I work as the assistant teacher for the Germantown Friends School after school robotics club every Wednesday for 45 minutes. The job requires me to assist 2nd to 5th grade students as they learn about the fundamental aspects of computer programming.
